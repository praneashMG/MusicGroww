    Getting Started

    How do I create an artist profile on Groww?

    Creating your artist profile takes just 10 minutes:

    1. Sign up with email or social media accounts
    2. Upload high-quality photos (min. 3 photos recommended)
    3. Add your music samples (MP3 format, max 10MB each)
    4. Set your availability calendar
    5. Define your performance rates and packages
    6. Verify your identity (required for payment processing)

    Pro Tip:

    Complete profiles get 3x more booking requests!

    Can I set my own prices and packages?

    Yes! You have full control over your pricing:

    Hourly Rate

    Set per-hour rates for different services

    Package Deals

    Create bundled offers (e.g., 3-hour wedding package)

    Custom Quotes

    Provide personalized quotes for special requests

    You can also set different rates for weekdays vs weekends, local vs travel gigs, and seasonal pricing.

    What happens if a client cancels a booking?

    Our cancellation policy protects both artists and clients:

    More than 30 days before Full refund
    15-30 days before 50% refund
    Less than 15 days before No refund

    Artists receive the non-refundable portion as compensation for holding the date. Force majeure exceptions apply.
