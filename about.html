    Our Journey

    From a simple idea to revolutionizing artist-platform relationships

    2018 The Beginning

    Founding Vision

    Three musicians envisioned a platform that truly understood artist needs. Started in a small garage studio with just $5,000 seed funding.

    2019 First Milestone

    Launch & Initial Growth

    Launched MVP with 50 pioneering artists. Secured first $1M in funding. Helped artists book over 1,000 gigs in first year.

    2020-2021 Pandemic Innovation

    Virtual Concerts & Expansion

    Pioneered virtual concert technology during lockdowns. Expanded to 25 new countries. Reached 5,000+ artist milestone.

    2022-Present Global Leadership

    Industry Transformation

    Introduced AI-powered artist matching. Partnered with major festivals worldwide. Became the fastest-growing artist platform globally.
