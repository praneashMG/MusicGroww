    Pricing FAQ

    Common questions about our pricing and billing

    Are there any setup fees or hidden charges?

    No! There are absolutely no setup fees, hidden charges, or surprise costs. The price you see is exactly what you pay. All features listed are included in your subscription, and we never charge extra for standard updates or maintenance.

    What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

    If you exceed your plan limits (like storage or upload limits), we'll notify you immediately. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional capacity at a pro-rated rate. We never shut off your service without warning, and you'll have 7 days to adjust your plan or usage.

    Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

    Yes! We offer special discounts for registered non-profit organizations, schools, universities, and educational institutions. These discounts typically range from 30-50% off depending on the organization type and size. Contact our sales team with verification documents to get your discount applied.

    Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all annual plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of an annual subscription, we'll provide a full refund. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime without penalty, but refunds are not typically provided for partial months used.
